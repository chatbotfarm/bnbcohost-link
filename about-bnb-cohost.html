About BNB CoHost

BNB CoHost is a boutique, full-service co-hosting and property management partner created for owners who want their short-term rentals cared for with intention, precision, and respect.

We specialize in working with established listings and discerning property owners who value thoughtful revenue strategy, seamless operations, and a guest experience that feels effortless — both for their guests and for themselves.

Strategic foundation, calm execution

Founded by a former IT Project Manager and Management Consultant, BNB CoHost brings a calm, structured approach to hosting. Before entering the short-term rental space, our founder partnered with state agencies and organizations across the DMV, helping teams streamline operations, manage complex projects, and make smart, data-driven decisions. That same strategic foundation now supports our clients’ real estate goals.

At BNB CoHost, we believe every property has its own rhythm and story. Our role is to honor that while quietly optimizing behind the scenes.
