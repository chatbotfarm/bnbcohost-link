FAQ

Who is BnB CoHost best suited for? ▾

We specialize in high-end, thoughtfully designed properties and work best with owners who value professionalism, transparency, and long-term performance over short-term gains.

What makes BnB CoHost different from traditional property managers? ▾

We don’t operate on volume. We curate.

Our approach blends Airbnb insider knowledge, hands-on operational excellence, and a hospitality-first mindset— delivering results while protecting your asset and brand.

Are you familiar with local regulations? ▾

Yes. Regulatory compliance is a cornerstone of our service. We help owners navigate licensing, zoning, tax obligations, and evolving short-term rental policies—particularly in the DMV region.

Do you only manage Airbnb listings? ▾

While Airbnb is our primary platform and area of deep expertise, we take a strategic, multi-platform view when appropriate— always prioritizing where your property performs best.

How involved do I need to be as an owner? ▾

As involved as you want to be. Some owners prefer full-service, hands-off management. Others enjoy visibility and collaboration. We tailor our approach to your preferences—without compromising standards.

How do you maintain a luxury guest experience? ▾

Through intentional design, proactive communication, vetted vendor partnerships, and operational precision. Luxury is not about excess—it’s about consistency, discretion, and care.
